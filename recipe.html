This recipe delivers a light, fluffy, and perfectly golden-brown pancakes every single time. They're a simple, delightful treat perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch. Be sure to use fresh ingredients for the best results!

Recipe Information

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 15 minutes
  • Number of servings: 4 servings (about 8-10 pancakes)
  • Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 Â½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 Â½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 Â¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter, plus extra for the griddle

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate, medium bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. A few lumps are okay; do not overmix!
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or large pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle to form pancakes of your desired size.
  6. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until bubbles start to appear on the surface and the edges look set. Flip and cook until golden brown.

Tips & Notes

Tip: Let the batter rest for 5-10 minutes before cooking. This helps the gluten relax and the baking powder activate, leading to an even fluffier texture!
